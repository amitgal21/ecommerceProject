What is Anime?

Anime refers to a style of animation that originated in Japan and has become popular worldwide. It is characterized by colorful artwork, fantastical themes, and vibrant characters. Anime spans a wide range of genres, from action and adventure to romance, sci-fi, and slice of life. Some of the most famous anime include "Naruto," "Dragon Ball Z," and "Attack on Titan."

What is Manga?

Manga is the Japanese word for comics or graphic novels. Manga serves as the source material for many popular anime series. It is read from right to left and is known for its detailed artwork and captivating stories. Manga covers a vast array of genres and is enjoyed by readers of all ages. Iconic manga series include "One Piece," "Bleach," and "My Hero Academia."
