Dragon Ball Series

Dragon Ball is one of the most iconic anime series of all time, created by Akira Toriyama. The series follows the adventures of Goku, a Saiyan warrior, and his friends as they defend Earth from powerful villains and search for the mystical Dragon Balls that grant wishes. With epic battles, deep friendships, and incredible transformations, Dragon Ball has become a beloved classic for fans of all ages. The series introduced iconic elements such as Super Saiyan forms, fusion techniques, and powerful antagonists like Frieza, Cell, and Majin Buu.
