Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a popular Japanese anime and manga series that delves into the world of sorcery and curses. The story follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who becomes involved with the Jujutsu Sorcerers after consuming a cursed object, the finger of Sukuna. The series is renowned for its intense battles, dark themes, and compelling character development.
